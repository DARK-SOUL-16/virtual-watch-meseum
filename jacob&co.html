Jacob&Co Watches

Founded:1986, New York City, USA

Founder: Jacob Arabo

History of Jacob&Co

Jacob&Co the brand began producing highly complex and luxurious timepieces, marking its transition from jewelry to fine horology. Jacob & Co. was known for creating unique designs that often pushed the boundaries of traditional watchmaking. The brand’s watches typically feature complicated movements and intricate craftsmanship, making them a standout in the luxury watch market.he brand became known for its bold, innovative designs and a reputation for extraordinary craftsmanship. Jacob & Co. watches became a symbol of luxury and status, attracting high-profile clientele, including celebrities, athletes, and business moguls.

10 Notable Jacob & Co.


1.Jacob & Co. Five Time Zone Watch

Material: Stainless steel or 18k gold,other-of-pearl, sapphire crystal, or enameled,scratch-resistant sapphire crystal,genuine leather or rubber

Made:2002

Price:$10,000 to $50,000+

Why It’s Popular:he Five Time Zone watch is one of the brand’s most iconic pieces, featuring five separate dials, each displaying the time in different time zones. It’s both a practical and statement-making piece, attracting attention for its bold design and multifunctional utility.


2.Jacob & Co. Astronomia Tourbillon

Material:18K white gold, sapphire crystal, diamonds

Made: 2014

Price:$600,000 to $1 Million+ (depending on the model and materials)

Why It’s Popular: The Astronomia Tourbillon is a technological marvel, combining a tourbillon with a rotating 3D dial that mimics the movement of the planets. Its sophisticated design and astronomical complications make it one of the brand’s most celebrated pieces.

3.Jacob & Co. Billionaire Watch

Material: 18K white gold, diamonds (over 260 carats)

Made: 2015

Price:$18 Million+

Why It’s Popular: The Billionaire Watch is a showcase of luxury, featuring over 260 carats of emerald-cut diamonds. It’s one of the most expensive watches ever made and is popular due to its extreme luxury appeal and the fact that it’s designed for the super-wealthy.

4.Jacob & Co. Epic X Tourbillon

Material: Titanium, black DLC coating, rubber

Made: 2016

Price:$50,000 to $100,000+

Why It’s Popular: The Epic X Tourbillon is a sporty luxury watch with a tourbillon movement and a modern design. Its use of titanium and rubber gives it a robust, contemporary look while maintaining Jacob & Co.'s trademark luxury craftsmanship.

5.Jacob & Co. Grand Complication Masterpieces

Material: 18K Rose Gold, Platinum, or White Gold (varies by specific edition),Sapphire crystal,High-quality alligator leather or matching precious metal bracelet.

Made: 2015

Price:$1 Million-$2 Million+

Why It’s Popular: The Grand Complication Masterpieces features a complex movement that includes multiple complications such as a perpetual calendar, minute repeater, and tourbillon. This watch is a true masterpiece of watchmaking and caters to collectors looking for extreme craftsmanship.

6.Jacob & Co. Twin Turbo Furious

Material: Titanium, rubber, leather

Made: 2019

Price: $500,000+

Why It’s Popular: The Twin Turbo Furious features two turbo-charged chronograph movements and skeletonized dial, offering a unique design and incredible mechanical complexity. It’s a tribute to speed and technology, catering to motorsport enthusiasts and collectors.

7.Jacob & Co. Bugatti Chiron Tourbillon

Material:18K rose gold, sapphire, leather

Made: 2019

Price:$1.5 Million

Why It’s Popular: This watch was designed in collaboration with Bugatti and features an incredible tourbillon mechanism and a unique design that mimics the features of the Bugatti Chiron supercar. Its luxury automotive connection and mechanical sophistication make it a standout piece.

8.Jacob & Co. Opera Godfather

Material: 18K white gold, diamonds, sapphire crystal

Made: 2016

Price:$2 Million+

Why It’s Popular: The Opera Godfather features a mechanical movement with a miniature music box that plays a tune when activated. Its intricate design and the inclusion of music make it a unique fusion of watchmaking and art.

9.Jacob & Co. Rose Gold and Diamond Astronomia

Material: 18K rose gold, diamonds, sapphire crystal

Made: 2014

Price: $500,000+

Why It’s Popular: The Astronomia collection is known for its complexity, and this version adds an opulent rose gold and diamond setting, making it a true luxury statement piece. It’s a favorite among collectors who want a bold, show-stopping watch.

10.Jacob & Co. Ghost

Material: 18K rose gold case, sapphire crystal, alligator leather strap

Made: 2019

Price:$150,000+

Why It’s Popular: The Jacob & Co. Ghost features a minimalist yet modern design. It has a skeletonized movement and a highly intricate dial that gives the watch a ghostly appearance. Its sleek design and high-tech features make it a favorite among those who appreciate both style and sophistication.
