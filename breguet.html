Breguet Watches

Founded: 1775

Founder: Abraham-Louis Breguet

History of Audemars Piguet

Abraham-Louis Breguet is often considered the "father of modern horology." He revolutionized watchmaking with several groundbreaking inventions, including the tourbillon, perpetual calendar, and Breguet hands (the unique style of watch hands). In addition to mechanical innovations, he was known for his work with royal patrons, including Queen Marie Antoinette, and for creating some of the earliest precision timepieces. After his death in 1823, the company continued to flourish and became a major name in high-end watchmaking, still revered today.

10 Notable Breguet Watches


1.Breguet Classique 5177

Material:18K yellow gold, silvered gold dial

Made: 2000s (modern design, introduced in 2004)

Price: $15,000 to $30,000+

Why It’s Popular: The Breguet Classique 5177 is an example of understated elegance with a clean, classic design. It features a simple yet sophisticated aesthetic, paying homage to Breguet's signature features like the guilloché dial and Breguet hands.


2.Breguet Marine 5817

Material:Stainless steel, titanium, 18K rose gold

Made:2005

Price: $50,000 to $100,000+ (depending on the model and materials)

Why It’s Popular: The Tradition 7027 is one of Breguet’s most recognizable models, known for its "inverted" movement where the bridges and gears are displayed on the dial. Its intricate design and the use of classic Breguet elements make it a favorite among collectors.


3.Breguet Tradition 7027

Material: 18K rose gold, white gold

Made: 1993

Price: $50,000 to $100,000+

Why It’s Popular: The Tradition 7027 is one of Breguet’s most recognizable models, known for its "inverted" movement where the bridges and gears are displayed on the dial. Its intricate design and the use of classic Breguet elements make it a favorite among collectors.


4.Breguet Type XX Aeronavale

Material: Stainless steel, 18K rose gold

Made: 1950s (reissued in the 2000s)

Price:$40,000 - $70,000 (varies based on model and materials)

Why It’s Popular: Originally designed for French naval aviators, the Type XX is famous for its chronograph function and military pedigree. Its historical significance and versatile design make it a popular piece.


5.Breguet Queen of Naples

Material: 18K yellow gold, diamonds, mother-of-pearl dial

Made: 2000s (launched in 2002)

Price: $40,000 to $150,000+

Why It’s Popular: Named after Queen Caroline Murat, the Queen of Naples is one of Breguet’s most iconic women’s watches, renowned for its unique, oval-shaped case and luxurious materials, along with its elegant design.


6.Breguet Classique 3330

Material: 18K white gold, platinum

Made:2000s

Price: $20,000 to $50,000+

Why It’s Popular: The 3330 features an elegant design with Breguet's signature guilloché pattern and a striking moonphase complication. It's beloved for its classical design and technical complexity.


7.Breguet Tourbillon 5347

Material: 18K white gold, titanium

Made: 2000s

Price:$100,000 to $200,000+

Why It’s Popular: As one of Breguet’s most prestigious models, the 5347 features a tourbillon and a chronograph, two of the most complicated mechanisms in horology. It showcases Breguet’s mastery of high-end watchmaking and remains a sought-after collector’s piece.


8. Breguet Classique 5157

Material: 18K yellow gold, platinum

Made:2000s

Price: $15,000 to $30,000+

Why It’s Popular:The Classique 5157 is a simple yet stunning dress watch with a clean dial and signature Breguet styling. Its combination of elegance and functionality makes it a go-to piece for collectors who appreciate understated luxury.


9. Breguet Classique 5377

Material: Platinum, 18K rose gold

Made: 2010s

Price: $50,000-$100,000+

Why It’s Popular:Known for its ultra-thin profile and advanced movement, the Classique 5377 is a testament to Breguet’s precision and watchmaking expertise. The watch is equipped with a high-tech movement and is highly prized by collectors for its thinness and craftsmanship.


10.Breguet Marine Chronograph 5823

Material: Stainless steel, titanium, rubber

Made: 2000s

Price: $ $20,000 to $40,000+

Why It’s Popular: This model features a chronograph complication and is built for performance with water resistance up to 100 meters. Its blend of luxury and sportiness appeals to those who want a versatile yet prestigious timepiece.
